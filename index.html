Hello, I'm Alan.

I bridge the gap between UX and development. I have 25 years experience in creating, launching and maintaining web applications and websites.

I am particularly interested in information architecture (IA) and the functional user experience (UX) design of complex workflows in long-life applications.

Currently, I spend my time leading the user experience of web and mobile applications at fintech startup Tongo Financial.

You can read about my full work experience in my resume, available as a PDF. It tells you all about my previous lives.

Since 2014, I have specialised in User Experience and Interaction Design. I have worked on several enterprise-sized web applications:

Tongo Financial

At Tongo, I was part of the team that designed and built the initial versions of the applications that the business runs on: a Dashboard through which customers sign up and manage their accounts; a back-of-office admin system used by admins, underwriters and customer support agents to support the business; and most recently I have produced designs for the iOS and Android apps.

Studio Designer

Studio Core, the accounting and project management software, at Studio Designer in California, plus sister products like a vendor order management system and a client self-service application

ACGI Software

Association management and certification software at ACGI Software in Maryland.

Regent Education

A SaaS suite for managing and dispersing financial aid to students for Regent Education in rural Maryland, as well as their public-facing student finance calculator

Where I Work

I have been happily working remotely for most of the last eight years. I live in beautiful Yellow Springs, OH with my wife, five cats and a pit bull.
